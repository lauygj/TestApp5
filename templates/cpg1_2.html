The prognosis from a CPG1 cancer is very good and your risk of dying of prostate cancer is likely to be much less compared to your risk of dying of other causes. The risk that the cancer may spread (metastasis) is also very low and uncommon. Immediate treatment is considered unlikely to confer much benefit to you. The National Institute for Health Care Excellence currently recommends active surveillance as the preferred management option for CPG1 cancer.
